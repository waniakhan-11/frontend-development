FAQs

What product can businesses purchase, and How are they different?

Both of our products give your company or team one-year of unlimited access to the entire MasterClass content library. The key differences pertain to method of delivery, administration, and use case: Licenses: Memberships are delivered through a central admin panel for distribution, which gives your administrator control over seat assignment (e.g. ability to add or remove users at any time). The license product also has deep reporting and analytics capabilities, and is perfect for complementing your learning and development programs. Codes: Memberships are delivered as a bulk list of unique codes. Once redeemed, each of these codes will be converted into a personal account by the redeemer and there is no requirement for a central administrator. These are great for employee or customer gifts. Still have questions? Please contact sales so we can assist you further!

I would like offer MasterClass to my employees as perk/benefit. Which should I purchase?

Both enterprise products can be effective, depending on your needs. The License product gives more flexibility in provisioning user access to MasterClass, and also includes engagement reporting capabilities to better assist your ongoing perks/benefits strategy. Alternatively, the Gifts product is a better fit if you’re looking to provide a one-time perk/benefit with minimal administrative overhead. Still have questions? Please contact sales so we can assist you further!

How does distribution work?

Licenses: You will be able to control distribution from a central admin panel, which will allow you to add and remove users at any time (by assigning emails to seats). Codes: You will receive an email with a CSV file containing unique code URLs, which will allow recipients to create an account and redeem a one-year membership. Still have questions? Please contact sales so we can assist you further!
